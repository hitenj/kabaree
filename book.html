Book Scrap Pickup

The Kabaree is owned by EnviCure WM Services LLP. and primarly based in Agra City.

We provide door to door service which intends to help out people to sell variety of scrap items

such as newspaper, cartoon, plastic, book, copy, metal etc and get paid a fair amount for it.

Just a few simple steps and our pickup man will arrive at your home, office etc at your convenient date. Book your appointment through our mobile app and sell any type of scrap, hassle free.

Sell your scrap in 4 easy steps:

  • Fill your basic details such as Name, Mobile Number, Address and Scrap Detail(Optional).
  • Our team will contact you for the confirmation of order and finalizing the pickup time, date and location.
  • The pickup boy will arrive at your home and he will bring along with him an electronic weighing machine.
  • You will be paid immediately after your scrap has been picked up.
