About Us

The Kabaree is owned by EnviCure WM Services LLP. and primarly based in Agra City.

We provide door to door service which intends to help out people to sell variety of scrap items

such as newspaper, cartoon, plastic, book, copy, metal etc
and get paid a fair amount for it.

Just a few simple steps and our pickup man will arrive at your home, office etc at your convenient date. Book your appointment through our mobile app and sell any type of scrap, hassle free.

Sell your scrap through the Kabaree Service in 4 easy steps:

  • Fill your basic details such as Name, Mobile Number, Address and Scrap Detail(Optional).
  • Our team will contact you for the confirmation of order and finalizing the pickup time, date and location.
  • The pickup boy will arrive at your home and he will bring along with him an electronic weighing machine.
  • You will be paid immediately after your scrap has been picked up.

Our objective is to recycle, reuse scrap thereby making our contribution in the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and also ensuring protection of the environment and cleanliness of the society.

FAQs | Frequently Asked Questions

Kabaree - FAQs

  • What is the service coverage area of Kabaree?

    As of now, we are working to full potential in and around Agra city, soon we will expand our wings in other towns and cities.

  • What modes of transactions are available for getting the value for scrap collected?

    For a seamless secured transaction experience, we recommend using online transaction mechanisms like Internet banking, UPI applications, etc.

  • How the price of scrap is explored and is it static or dynamic?

    As we handle a wide variety of scraps, price depends on the type of scrap. The price of a few grades of scrap is dynamic based on market conditions. Hence price list in Kabaree is updated on daily basis. Each time before placing the order we request you to go through the price list to get a better estimate.

  • How can I reach the Kabaree team for any issue, is there any contact number or email to reach Kabaree?

    We are happy to serve your enquiries, you can write to us at thekabaree@gmail.com or dial us at +91 97597 11222.

  • In case of a business entity, will the invoice include a GST number?

    We do provide an Invoice with GST details.

  • How will you weigh the scrap collected, will it be weighed on the pickup site or in the warehouse?

    Our scrap pickup vehicles are provisioned with a standard weighing scale hence we will weigh the scrap on the pickup site itself. If in case of heavily weighed industrial scrap we will get it weighed in a warehouse facility after getting consent from the customer.

  • How will you sort the scrap, should the customer sort it or Kabaree executive will sort the scrap?

    As we offer rates for scrap based on its type and grades, we recommend our customers to sort it so that the pickup happens smooth and quick. Incase small quantity of unsorted scrap, we do have separate pricing for that too. Anyway, our Kabaree executive will be kind enough in helping you out during the scrap pickup.

  • How Kabaree is managing hygiene and safety in picking up scrap during COVID times?

    At Kabaree we understand the importance of maintaining hygiene in our day to day business, we do regular temperature checks for our field executives, We provision hygiene kits in all our vehicles which includes sanitizer sprays to be used in each pickup.
